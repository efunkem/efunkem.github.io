FAQs


How do I play?

Its simple! First, create an Ardor account so that you can receive your funds. Next, solve the puzzle. Finally, enter your account ID and click enter!

How do I receive my winnings?

The solution to the crossword is a private key to a cryptocurrency wallet set up by CryptoCrosswords.com. Solving the puzzle automatically submits a transaction that sends the contents of the wallet to your account.

How often are new crosswords released?

There is no set schedule. Roughly once per week or when existing crosswords are solved.

How can I be notified when new puzzles are released?

Join the email newsletter to be the first to know! You'll find out about new crosswords as fast as possible to give yourself a head start.

Do I have to submit my email to play?

Nope. You don't have to submit your email or ANY personal data to play or to receive your winnings. You can stay completely anonymous. That's part of the magic of cryptocurrencies.

What cryptocurrencies can I win?

Current offerings are Ardor and Ignis.

Do you have plans to expand to other cryptocurrencies?

Potentially, depending on popularity and demand.
